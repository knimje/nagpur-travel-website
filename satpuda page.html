Satpuda Botanical Garden

Satpura Botanical Garden located on the Seminary Hills,
near Phutala Lake is a splendid garden in Nagpur attracting
thousands of people. Satpura botanical garden houses numerous
rare species of plants. The garden is an excellent place for
those who are studying biology, zoology and environment science
as it provide information about various aspects of tree morphology,
phrenology and regeneration and tree architecture. The garden and
its surroundings are the favorite among the bird enthusiasts.
