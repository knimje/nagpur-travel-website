Khindsi Lake

Khindsi, the picturesque and large lake surrounded by verdant
forests on all sides is located about 3.5kms from Ramtek and 53kms
from Nagpur. It has been the favourite tourist destination of
Vidarbhites for several years. Tourist can enjoy boating in Khindsi
lake in motorboats, pedal boats, rowing boats, water scooters etc.
Nearby the lake, there is an adventure park for the children. For the
adventure seekers, jungle trekking is also available.
