Totladoh

Totladoh dam, is a gravity dam on Pench river near
Ramtek in Nagpur district 80 km. from Nagpur on N.H. No.-7.
This dam, a part of the Pench River hydroelectric project,
sits amidst a scenic wonderland, where blue water and emerald
green forest come together in an almost picture-postcard setting.
