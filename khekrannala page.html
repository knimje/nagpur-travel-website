Khekranala Lake

Khekranala, situated 65 km north of Nagpur in Khapa range
is a beautiful dam site. Nestled on the Chhindwara Road near
Khapa, Khekranala is an ideal spot for adventure seekers,
specially for trekking. The major attraction here is the tranquil
lake around the Khekranala Dam, surrounded by a dense forest with
wide variety of flora and fauna. The lush greenery, healthy climate
and fascinating environment attracts tourists from different parts
of the state. Maharahtra Tourism Development Corporation (MTDC)
has developed lodging facilities for the visitors.
